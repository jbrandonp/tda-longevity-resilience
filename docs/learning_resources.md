# Learning Resources — Topological Data Analysis

## Beginner

1. **"Topology and Data" — Gunnar Carlsson (2009)**
   - Classic survey introducing persistent homology and Mapper. *Bull. AMS*.
   - [PDF](https://www.ams.org/journals/bull/2009-46-02/S0273-0979-09-01249-X/)

2. **AATRN (Applied Algebraic Topology Research Network)**
   - [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@AATRN_) with tutorials, seminars, and workshops.
   - Excellent for self-study.

3. **"The Shape of Data" — Elizabeth Munch**
   - Accessible introduction to TDA concepts. [Video lecture](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_fNKrVPxQQE).

## Intermediate

4. **"Computational Topology for Data Analysis" — Dey & Wang (2022)**
   - Comprehensive textbook covering algorithms and applications. Cambridge University Press.

5. **Scikit-TDA Tutorials**
   - [https://scikit-tda.org](https://scikit-tda.org) — tutorials for ripser, persim, kmapper.

6. **"Topological Machine Learning" — Hensel, Moor, Rieck (2021)**
   - Survey of ML methods using topological features. *JMLR*.

## Advanced

7. **"Persistence Theory: From Quiver Representations to Data Analysis" — Oudot (2015)**
   - Mathematical foundations of persistent homology. AMS.

8. **"An Introduction to Topological Data Analysis" — Chazal & Michel (2021)**
   - Rigorous treatment with statistical guarantees. *Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence*.
